# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in ComposeBastion.

ComposeBastion is source-available but not open source.

Free use is allowed for home labs, home use, private use, personal learning,
and private non-commercial testing.

Business or organizational use requires prior written approval or a purchased
license.

At this stage, code contributions are not accepted unless explicitly approved by
the project maintainers.

You may open issues for:

- bug reports;
- feature suggestions;
- documentation suggestions;
- security concerns, if they do not expose sensitive details publicly.

Please do not submit code, pull requests, patches, or large implementation
details unless requested.

If code contributions are accepted in the future, contributors may be required
to sign a Contributor License Agreement before their contribution can be merged.

By submitting ideas, feedback, issue reports, or non-code suggestions, you
grant the project maintainers permission to use them without restriction or
compensation.

For support or licensing questions, contact:

https://github.com/composebastion-admin/composebastion/issues
